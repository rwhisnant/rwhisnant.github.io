BIO

My name is Rebecca Whisnant. I am 23 years old and a junior at Gardner-Webb University. I will be finishing in the Fall of 2019 with a Computer Science bachelors degree. I designed this organization off of personal reasons. I was given the idea to start R.A.D Designs after listening to Tyler Perry's message at Joel Olsten's church. He gave the scripture Genesis 22:1-14 KJV and told us a story of how this scripture helped him over come anxiety on the air plane. God gave him the message "Climb and Maintain". 'climb' came from a term for the plane when they were having difficulties and he began to pray. The difficulties were over and he got the message "Pray and Worship" the term 'worship' came from him over coming that so now he owns he's own airplane. So with that being said I decided to look more into scriptures to help me with my struggles in school. I even spoke to my grandmother and she gave me a good scripture she follows Psalm 37 KJV. God gave her the message "Fret Not". It means do not worry. Learning and reading up on scriptures are making me stronger everyday. I believe that I have been given an opportunity to spread the gospel and what I have learned to help me and others. In doing so, R.A.D Design is a non-profit organization which means 25 percent of our profit goes to different charitable organizations every month. R.A.D Designs mission is to uplift people and give back to surrounding communities. Products are made via free trade. Personalized aspect from manufacturers (Us-R.A.D Designs) .Thank you for visiting our website and hope you enjoyed our page as much as we enjoy spreading the gospel one design at a time.
